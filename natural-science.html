Natural Science Curriculum

Exploring Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics.

1. Biology

Biology is the study of life and living organisms. It explores the structure, growth, origin, evolution, and distribution of living things, including humans, plants, animals, and microorganisms.

2. Chemistry

Chemistry investigates the properties, composition, and structure of matter. It examines how different substances interact, combine, and change to form new substances through chemical reactions.

3. Physics

Physics is the science of energy, motion, light, sound, and the fundamental interactions between matter. It is the key to understanding the laws that govern our universe.

4. Mathematics

While Mathematics is its own field, it serves as the essential tool in Natural Science. It is vital for the complex calculations and modeling required in Physics and Chemistry.

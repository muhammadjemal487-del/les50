Social Science Department

Understanding Society, History, and Global Economics.

1. History & Civics

Explore the journey of civilizations and the evolution of human governance. We teach students the importance of heritage, national identity, and the duties of a citizen.

2. Economics

Dive into the world of finance and resource management. Students learn about market dynamics, international trade, and the systems that drive global wealth.

3. Geography

Understand our planet's physical structures and climate patterns. We explore the relationship between human activity and the environment in the 21st century.

4. Law & Sociology

Study the legal frameworks and social behaviors that shape our world. This course prepares future leaders for careers in legal practice and social research.
